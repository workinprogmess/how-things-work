## Diagram Descriptions

1. **Comprehensive Taxonomy & Therapy Pathway** - Complete flowchart covering identification, assessment, diagnosis, treatment planning, intervention, and progress monitoring for child speech sound disorders (ages 2-8)

2. **Indian SLP Training Pathway** - Educational and career progression for Speech-Language Pathologists in India, from high school through Ph.D.

3. **Clinical Decision Tree** - Step-by-step diagnostic decision flowchart for classifying speech disorders and selecting appropriate therapy approaches

## Features in Mermaid Live Editor

- **Pan**: Click and drag to move around the diagram
- **Zoom**: Use mouse wheel or zoom buttons
- **Export**: Download as SVG or PNG
- **Share**: Copy the URL to share with others
- **Edit**: Modify the diagram if needed
