## Diagram Descriptions

1. **Comprehensive Pediatric OT Pathway** - Complete flowchart covering identification, assessment, diagnosis, intervention planning, intervention delivery, and outcome measurement for children with developmental or participation challenges (ages 2-12 years)

2. **Indian OT Training Pathway** - Educational and career progression for Occupational Therapists in India, from high school through Ph.D.

3. **Clinical Decision Tree** - Step-by-step intervention selection flowchart based on primary concerns (sensory, motor, handwriting, self-care, complex)
