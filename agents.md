## commit guidelines

- commit as often as possible
- format:
  - title: very specific, lowercase, no signatures
  - body: 'what' statements in bullets + 'why' section
  - never use claude or conductor signatures in message
- everything in lowercase: docs, files, code, commit messages, conversations
  - exception: language-specific conventions (swift variables/functions follow their guidelines)

research project focused on:

- organizing in proper folder structure with sub-folders and files
